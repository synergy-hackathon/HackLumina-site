HackLumina-25

About

HackLumina, a 36-hour hackathon for high school students passionate about technology, innovation, and exploring the world of computers.We'll bring together almost 150 high school students from the Delhi NCR and surrounding region.Participants will immerse themselves in hands-on learning, workshops, and exploration of computers and technology in a high-energy environment. Your support would mean everything to us, as we aim to inspire the next generation of tech enthusiasts.
